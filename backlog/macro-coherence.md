## Detector architecture

- **MC-08 · DONE · scanner/src/suppression/api.rs + engine/process.rs** — the gating is now keyed on capture **shape**, not on the `id.starts_with("generic-")` proxy. `detector_weak_anchor(spec)` (api.rs) STRUCTURALLY derives "weakly anchored" from the detector's own regex: `has_broad_identifier_capture` flags any `([a-zA-Z0-9_-]+|*|{0,1})`-style full-alpha capture with a ≤1 minimum (Category-C of FP_AUDIT — matches any short identifier after the keyword anchor), so every present AND future detector with that shape is covered without a hand-list. The one class shape genuinely CANNOT separate — pure-hex (`[a-f0-9]{32}`/`{40}`, byte-identical to a real Algolia/etc. hex key) — stays in the small corpus-derived `RESIDUAL_WEAK_ANCHORED` set, documented as a deliberate measurement-driven exception (shape alone is provably insufficient there). The entropy floor + camelCase gate (`process.rs:128`) and the Tier-B shape filters (pure-identifier / word-identifier / scheme-URI / colliding-punctuation / url-path / UUID / b64-blob) now fire for `is_generic || is_weakly_anchored`, i.e. for shape-classified weak named detectors, NOT just `generic-*`. The remaining `id.starts_with("generic-"/"entropy-")` checks are the EXACT identity of the no-anchor fallback class (definitionally anchorless — not a shape proxy), and a strongly-anchored named detector keeps its bypass because its service regex IS positive evidence (a 32-hex after `ALGOLIA_ADMIN_KEY=` is an Algolia key, not an MD5). Universal always-fire gates (`prefixed_hash_digest` `sha256:…`, `dashed_serial`, syntactic-punctuation, email, regex-tail, vendored-minified, binary-strings) catch labelled digests/serials even on strongly-anchored detectors. Pinned by `tests/gaps/detector_precision_decoys.rs` (62 tests, all green): every ambiguous shape is suppressed on the generic/weak path AND its real twin survives, and the SAME shape survives under a strongly-anchored id — a bidirectional contract impossible to pass with an always-true gate. (audit FP_AUDIT 7 HIGH + 290 MED)

(1) The detector SPEC schema is a single `DetectorSpec` (+ nested `PatternSpec`/`CompanionSpec`/`VerifySpec`/…), all `#[serde(deny_unknown_fields)]` (typo-guard covers the whole file), in `core/src/spec.rs`. Locked by `core/tests/gap/every_embedded_detector_parses.rs` (every embedded TOML parses via `DetectorFile`) + `embedded_parseable_count_equals_slice_len` (no embed-time drop) + `new_core_allowlist_spec.rs::load_detectors_from_str_rejects_unknown_field` (schema is strict/authoritative), and embed-vs-disk drift is caught by the MC-06 detector digest.
